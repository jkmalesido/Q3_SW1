Matilda

1996



Summary:

An American fantasy comedy film about a young, gifted girl with a keen intellect and psychic powers who uses them to deal with her crude, distant family and free her kindhearted teacher from her school's sadistic headmistress.







Matilda Wormwood

Matilda is the main protagonist of the film, 'Matilda.' At an early age, she had to learn how to survive on her own due to her negligent family. She is a gifted intellectual who loves to read books starting at an early age. When she discovered that she had psychic powers, she uses them to deal with her family and school-life.




Miss Honey

Miss Honey serves as a motherlike figure for Matilda. She is the children's school teacher and known as sweet, generous, and loving. She lives in a tiny cottage under her oppressive aunt.



Agatha Trunchbull

Agatha Trunchbull is the tyrannical headmistress at Matilda's school. She is known to despise children and frightened the life out of her students. She is also rude, insulting, and abusive. Furthermore, she is Miss Honey's aunt or legal guardian.




Harry Wormwood

Harry Wormwood is Matilda's dismissive father. He calls her names and tears her books apart. His personality is known to be greedy, selfish, dishonest, and bad-tempered.




Zinnia Wormwood

Zinnia Wormwood is Matilda's careless mother and Harry Wormwood's wife. She is an obsessed bingo player and known for her unconventional opinions and sassy remarks. Similar to Michael, Zinnia is also greedy, selfish, and abusive.




Michael Wormwood

Michael Wormwood is Matilda's older brother by five years. He is known to be an ordinary boy who is not as intelligent as his sister, but a messy eater. Their father wants Michael to take over him in their business.




Lavender

Lavender is one of the students who was as smart as Matilda. She was small and skinny, with deep-brown eyes and dark hair. She was Matilda's kindhearted and caring best friend.
